Key Finding Discounting — not weak sales — is the company's core profitability problem. Orders with no discount run a 29.5% margin; orders discounted 31%+ lose 48 cents per dollar of sales. Furniture — Tables is the single worst sub-category, down $17.7K despite $207K in sales.
